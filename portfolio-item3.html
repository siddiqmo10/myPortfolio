QuizApp

A short quiz app to introduce my client's company

One of my first projects during my internship involved making a quiz web application, to be used by the commnunity management team, to introduce the company. I was instructed to follow a certain style that represent the 4th industrial revolution.

I used javascript for the functionality which included colour coded correct and wrong answers. I was also intsructed to assign background images to certain questions along and finally a button that led to a form, to subscribe that was also custom made to match the client's specifications.

One problem that I ran into involved cross browser compatibilty, mainly firefox had a different order of questions than the standard order. I planned on tackling the issue by targeting firefox and making it point to a different array of questions to presesnt the standard order which was my quick fix.
